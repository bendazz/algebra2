Composite Functions

Sum and Difference of Functions

1. Given \( f(x) = 3x - 2 \) and \( g(x) = x^2 + 1 \), find \( (f + g)(x) \).
Reveal Solution

Add the two functions:

$$ (f + g)(x) = f(x) + g(x) = (3x - 2) + (x^2 + 1) $$ $$ = x^2 + 3x - 1 $$

Answer: \( (f + g)(x) = x^2 + 3x - 1 \)

2. Given \( f(x) = 4x + 5 \) and \( g(x) = x^2 - 2x \), find \( (f - g)(x) \).
Reveal Solution

Subtract \( g \) from \( f \):

$$ (f - g)(x) = f(x) - g(x) = (4x + 5) - (x^2 - 2x) $$ $$ = 4x + 5 - x^2 + 2x = -x^2 + 6x + 5 $$

Answer: \( (f - g)(x) = -x^2 + 6x + 5 \)

Product of Functions

3. Given \( f(x) = 3x - 2 \) and \( g(x) = x^2 + 1 \), find \( (fg)(x) \).
Reveal Solution

Multiply the two functions:

$$ (fg)(x) = f(x) \cdot g(x) = (3x - 2)(x^2 + 1) $$

Distribute:

$$ = 3x \cdot x^2 + 3x \cdot 1 - 2 \cdot x^2 - 2 \cdot 1 $$ $$ = 3x^3 + 3x - 2x^2 - 2 $$ $$ = 3x^3 - 2x^2 + 3x - 2 $$

Answer: \( (fg)(x) = 3x^3 - 2x^2 + 3x - 2 \)

4. Given \( f(x) = 4x + 5 \) and \( g(x) = x^2 - 2x \), find \( (fg)(-1) \).
Reveal Solution

Evaluate each function at \( x = -1 \):

$$ f(-1) = 4(-1) + 5 = -4 + 5 = 1 $$ $$ g(-1) = (-1)^2 - 2(-1) = 1 + 2 = 3 $$

Multiply the results:

$$ (fg)(-1) = f(-1) \cdot g(-1) = 1 \cdot 3 = 3 $$

Answer: \( (fg)(-1) = 3 \)

Composition of Functions

5. Given \( f(x) = 3x - 2 \) and \( g(x) = x^2 + 1 \), find \( (f \circ g)(x) \).
Reveal Solution

Replace \( x \) in \( f \) with \( g(x) \):

$$ (f \circ g)(x) = f\bigl(g(x)\bigr) = f(x^2 + 1) $$ $$ = 3(x^2 + 1) - 2 = 3x^2 + 3 - 2 $$

Answer: \( (f \circ g)(x) = 3x^2 + 1 \)

6. Given \( f(x) = 3x - 2 \) and \( g(x) = x^2 + 1 \), find \( (g \circ f)(x) \).
Reveal Solution

Replace \( x \) in \( g \) with \( f(x) \):

$$ (g \circ f)(x) = g\bigl(f(x)\bigr) = g(3x - 2) $$ $$ = (3x - 2)^2 + 1 $$

Expand \( (3x - 2)^2 \):

$$ = 9x^2 - 12x + 4 + 1 $$

Answer: \( (g \circ f)(x) = 9x^2 - 12x + 5 \)

7. Given \( f(x) = 4x + 5 \) and \( g(x) = x^2 - 2x \), find \( (f \circ g)(3) \).
Reveal Solution

First evaluate \( g(3) \):

$$ g(3) = (3)^2 - 2(3) = 9 - 6 = 3 $$

Then evaluate \( f(3) \):

$$ f(3) = 4(3) + 5 = 12 + 5 = 17 $$

Answer: \( (f \circ g)(3) = 17 \)

8. Given \( f(x) = 4x + 5 \) and \( g(x) = x^2 - 2x \), find \( (g \circ f)(x) \).
Reveal Solution

Replace \( x \) in \( g \) with \( f(x) \):

$$ (g \circ f)(x) = g\bigl(f(x)\bigr) = g(4x + 5) $$ $$ = (4x + 5)^2 - 2(4x + 5) $$

Expand:

$$ = 16x^2 + 40x + 25 - 8x - 10 $$

Answer: \( (g \circ f)(x) = 16x^2 + 32x + 15 \)

Inverse Functions

9. Find the inverse of \( f(x) = 3x - 2 \).
Reveal Solution

Replace \( f(x) \) with \( y \), then swap \( x \) and \( y \):

$$ y = 3x - 2 \implies x = 3y - 2 $$

Solve for \( y \):

$$ x + 2 = 3y $$ $$ y = \frac{x + 2}{3} $$

Answer: \( f^{-1}(x) = \dfrac{x + 2}{3} \)

10. Find the inverse of \( f(x) = \dfrac{2x + 1}{x - 3} \).
Reveal Solution

Swap \( x \) and \( y \):

$$ x = \frac{2y + 1}{y - 3} $$

Multiply both sides by \( (y - 3) \):

$$ x(y - 3) = 2y + 1 $$ $$ xy - 3x = 2y + 1 $$

Collect \( y \)-terms on one side:

$$ xy - 2y = 3x + 1 $$ $$ y(x - 2) = 3x + 1 $$ $$ y = \frac{3x + 1}{x - 2} $$

Answer: \( f^{-1}(x) = \dfrac{3x + 1}{x - 2} \)

Composition with Inverses

11. Given \( f(x) = 3x - 2 \), verify that \( (f \circ f^{-1})(x) = x \).
Reveal Solution

From Problem 9, we know \( f^{-1}(x) = \dfrac{x + 2}{3} \). Compute \( f\bigl(f^{-1}(x)\bigr) \):

$$ f\!\left(\frac{x + 2}{3}\right) = 3 \cdot \frac{x + 2}{3} - 2 $$ $$ = (x + 2) - 2 = x \;\; \checkmark $$

Answer: \( (f \circ f^{-1})(x) = x \), confirming the inverse is correct.

12. Given \( f(x) = 4x + 5 \), find \( f^{-1}(x) \) and verify that \( (f^{-1} \circ f)(x) = x \).
Reveal Solution

Find the inverse: Swap \( x \) and \( y \):

$$ x = 4y + 5 \implies 4y = x - 5 \implies y = \frac{x - 5}{4} $$

So \( f^{-1}(x) = \dfrac{x - 5}{4} \).

Verify: Compute \( f^{-1}\bigl(f(x)\bigr) \):

$$ f^{-1}(4x + 5) = \frac{(4x + 5) - 5}{4} = \frac{4x}{4} = x \;\; \checkmark $$

Answer: \( f^{-1}(x) = \dfrac{x - 5}{4} \), and \( (f^{-1} \circ f)(x) = x \).
