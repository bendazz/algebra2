Expanding Logarithms

Key Properties:
Product Rule: \( \log_b(MN) = \log_b(M) + \log_b(N) \)
Quotient Rule: \( \log_b\!\left(\dfrac{M}{N}\right) = \log_b(M) - \log_b(N) \)
Power Rule: \( \log_b(M^c) = c \cdot \log_b(M) \)

Expanding Products

1. Expand: \( \log_2(3xy^3) \)
Reveal Solution

Apply the product rule to separate the factors:

$$ \log_2(3) + \log_2(x) + \log_2(y^3) $$

Apply the power rule to \( \log_2(y^3) \):

Answer: \( \log_2(3) + \log_2(x) + 3\log_2(y) \)

2. Expand: \( \log_4(5x^3 y^2 z) \)
Reveal Solution

Apply the product rule to separate all factors:

$$ \log_4(5) + \log_4(x^3) + \log_4(y^2) + \log_4(z) $$

Apply the power rule to each term with an exponent:

Answer: \( \log_4(5) + 3\log_4(x) + 2\log_4(y) + \log_4(z) \)

Expanding Quotients

3. Expand: \( \log_5\!\left(\dfrac{x^2}{y}\right) \)
Reveal Solution

Apply the quotient rule:

$$ \log_5(x^2) - \log_5(y) $$

Apply the power rule to \( \log_5(x^2) \):

Answer: \( 2\log_5(x) - \log_5(y) \)

4. Expand: \( \log\!\left(\dfrac{7x^4}{z^3}\right) \)
Reveal Solution

Apply the quotient rule:

$$ \log(7x^4) - \log(z^3) $$

Apply the product rule to the numerator:

$$ \log(7) + \log(x^4) - \log(z^3) $$

Apply the power rule:

Answer: \( \log(7) + 4\log(x) - 3\log(z) \)

Roots and Combined Expressions

5. Expand: \( \ln\!\left(\sqrt{x^3 y}\right) \)
Reveal Solution

Rewrite the square root as an exponent of \( \frac{1}{2} \):

$$ \ln\!\left((x^3 y)^{1/2}\right) $$

Apply the power rule:

$$ \frac{1}{2}\,\ln(x^3 y) $$

Apply the product rule inside:

$$ \frac{1}{2}\bigl[\ln(x^3) + \ln(y)\bigr] $$

Apply the power rule to \( \ln(x^3) \) and distribute:

$$ \frac{1}{2}\bigl[3\ln(x) + \ln(y)\bigr] $$

Answer: \( \dfrac{3}{2}\ln(x) + \dfrac{1}{2}\ln(y) \)

6. Expand: \( \log_3\!\left(\dfrac{2x^2 y^5}{z^4}\right) \)
Reveal Solution

Apply the quotient rule:

$$ \log_3(2x^2 y^5) - \log_3(z^4) $$

Apply the product rule to expand the numerator:

$$ \log_3(2) + \log_3(x^2) + \log_3(y^5) - \log_3(z^4) $$

Apply the power rule to each term:

Answer: \( \log_3(2) + 2\log_3(x) + 5\log_3(y) - 4\log_3(z) \)
